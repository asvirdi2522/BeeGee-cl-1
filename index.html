Hello everybody!

Hi, I'm Jahnvi, an independent artist from India. As I've been painting and drawing since I was very young, I decided to turn my passion into my career. My medium of choice are oils and posters, and I choose to express myself through these colours. Whenever there's a brush in my hand, I know there's magic just waiting to happen. There are no rules, only endless inspiration. I believe in working closely with my clients to create artwork that fits their vision. You imagine, I create!
